Loan Duration

  • Asset Finance Short term loans of up to 1 year with an option to be backed up by collateral e.g. sureties,cessions etc.
  • Bridging Loans Linked to term of the contract,up to 12 months.
  • Revolving Loans Repayment term is up to a period of 12 months or dependent on the duration of the contract
  • Term Loans Repayment period is between 12 and 60 months

Loans or financing will NOT be offered to

  • Companies engaging in speculative real estate,tobacco,liquor,gambling or sex trade
  • Business involved in the trade of illegal goods or services
  • Individuals or businesses whose director(s) or other senior management member is insolvent or under debt review

Loans or financing will be offered to

  • Businesses that consist of sole trader with reliable physical address
  • Individuals with character and ability to repay loan and have completed the necessary application forms
  • Businesses that have written proposals or business plan that meets the requirements of our loan application criteria
  • Have supplied required supporting documentation with required personal and credit references

How to apply

  • Business must consist of sole trader with reliable physical address
  • Proven personal and credit references
  • Required contractual capacity
  • Must be owner manager
  • Character and ability to repay loan
