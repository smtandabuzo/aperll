Asset Finance

We offer asset finance that is tailored for whatever your business needs might be, covering new and used moveable assets.

You can use asset finance for the purchase of equipment and machines(identified by serial numbers) that are important in the day to day running of your business. When you apply for this option you can rest assured that your business will function optimally, having all the equipment and machinery required without impacting your bank balance. You also have the option to lease or rent the asset if this would better suit your business need and/or budget

The repayment term (i.e. how long you have to finish paying the loan)is either 60 months or the amount of time the asset is deemed to be economical. Important to note, AIDC can use your acquired asset as security for the loan

Bridging Loans

This is a loan or financing for a business to fund its day to day operations providing for items such as stock and operating overheads.

If you run a business that transacts or have contracts with other businesses and require immediate funding for these operations, this is the option for you. The repayment term of the loan is equal to the duration of the contract you have, up to a period of 12 months.

Revolving Loans

This is an option where the entity borrowing can use the funds as and when needed. This is suited for businesses with contracts of predefined monetary value and lifespan or term. The loan is used mainly for day to day operations of the business, with varying amounts loaned as is needed by the customer on a month to month basis.

Due to the nature of the loan,this option is only offerred or available to business with a proven track record and credit history. The loan is repayable for a duration of 12 months or for the contract term.

Term Loans

This type of loan can be used by businesses to get different forms of moveable assets. This is slightly different from asset finance as with this option you can buy moveable assets such as office supplies and furniture that cannot be identified by serial numbers.

The repayment term for this type of loan is between 12 and 60 months.
