How long after my application is approved will funds be available in my bank account?

It depends on the type of loan you are applying for. Approval is faster for bridging loans and revolving credit facility, no business plan is required just a valid contract. Term facilities take longer than bridging loans and need to be accompanied with a detailed business plan. It is recommended to submit all required documentation as listed in the application check list in order for your application to be approved as soon as possible.

Will my application for funding be approved if I am black-listed?

We consider all applications that we receive. If you are black-listed, you must however show willingness to recover from this state by making arrangements with the institutions or individuals you owe.

What type of businesses do you provide funding for?

We offer funding for varying types of businesses, be it an existing business you would like to own or one you would like to start.
